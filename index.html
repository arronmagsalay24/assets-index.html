About Us

Built by people who know hospitality, OrderGuard helps takeaways grow without relying on big apps. We understand the challenges independent restaurants face—high commission fees, limited control over customer relationships, and the struggle to stand out in crowded marketplaces.

That’s why we created OrderGuard: a simple yet powerful ordering system designed specifically for takeaways and restaurants. With your own branded website, real-time order management, and access to valuable customer insights, you stay in control while building loyalty and increasing profits.

Our mission is to empower local businesses to thrive, giving you the tools of big platforms without the hidden costs or restrictions.
